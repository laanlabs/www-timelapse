Welcome to iTimeLapse

By Laan Labs

iTimeLapse is the premier time-lapse photography application for iPhone and iPad. Create stunning time-lapse videos with professional features and intuitive controls.

Whether you're capturing clouds rolling across the sky, flowers blooming, or the hustle and bustle of city life, iTimeLapse gives you the power to create amazing time-lapse videos right from your iOS device.

Key Features

  • Adjustable interval timing for precise control
  • High-resolution video output
  • Built-in video stabilization
  • Export to camera roll or share directly
  • Intuitive interface designed for iOS
  • Professional-grade results

iTimeLapse is perfect for photographers, videographers, and anyone who wants to capture the world in a unique and creative way. Download it today from the App Store and start creating your own time-lapse masterpieces!
