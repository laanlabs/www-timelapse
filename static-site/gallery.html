User Creations

From sunrise to sunset, from bustling city streets to peaceful nature scenes, iTimeLapse users have captured it all. The app's intuitive interface and powerful features make it easy to create professional-quality time-lapse videos.

Tips for Great Time-Lapses

  • Use a stable tripod or mount for best results
  • Choose interesting subjects with visible movement
  • Pay attention to lighting conditions
  • Experiment with different interval settings
  • Keep your device charged throughout the shoot
  • Consider the final duration of your video
